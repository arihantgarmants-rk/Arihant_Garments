About Arihant Garments

Two Decades of Trust,
One Thread at a Time

Established in 2001, Arihant Garments is Kolkata's trusted school uniform manufacturer, supplying quality uniforms to schools and businesses across West Bengal and India. Operating from our production facility, we offer customised, competitively priced uniforms with fast fulfilment — backed by over two decades of consistent service.

Our philosophy is simple: relationships over transactions, quality over compromise, and transparency in everything we do. Arihant Garments is more than a uniform supplier — we are a long-term partner you can rely on, season after season.
